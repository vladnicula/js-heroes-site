Cluj has few public transport options. The buses are operated by CTP. This is the schedule of line 21 that takes you from the city center, to the conference venue area.

Taxis in Cluj are affordable and everywhere. You can usually hail one in the street, or use apps like CleverTaxi to order one. Also, for travelling inside Cluj-Napoca, we definitely recommend Pony car sharing.

Biking is difficult since Cluj has few bicycle lanes and the conference location is up on one of the hills surrounding the city.
